Menu Bar

Images

Click on the actual image to include it into the export folder.
Double click on the actual image to copy it to the left side image.

Image in Albums

This bar shows all albums, the actual image is in.

Control Bar

Jump to the first, previos, next or last image.

Albums Overview

Click on an album button to show only images of this album. The album button switches to selected.
Click on a selected album button to unselect this album. Images of all albums will be shown.

Click with the secondary mouse button on an album button to delete this album.
